  # rebuilt-strategy
---

a simulator that takes assumptions and gives out graphs of point values (x-value) vs. how often those values were achieved (y-value).
assumptions:
| item | time (estimated) | time (variation) | porportion of success |
| ------ | ------- | -------- | ------ |
Climb L1	| 5 sec	| 2 sec	| 1
Climb l2	| 10 sec	| 2 sec	| 1
Climb l3	| 15 sec	| 2 sec	| 1
Ground pickup 8 fuel	| 10 seconds	| 5 sec	| 1
Ground pickup 15 fuel	| 20 sec	| 7 sec	| 1
Shoot 8 fuel (auto align) |	5 seconds	| 1 sec	| 1
Shoot 15 fuel (auto align)	| 8 sec	| 1 sec	| 1
Shoot 8 fuel (manual align)	| 7 sec	| 2.5 sec	| 1
Shoot 15 fuel (manual align)	| 10 sec | 2.5 sec | 1
